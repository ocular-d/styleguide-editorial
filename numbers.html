If you are adding numbers to a table, use numerals even for numbers 1-9. Tables are meant to provide fast access to content for analysis.

# Big Numbers

When you are writing big numbers (anything that is larger than a thousand), you can write it either way:

  • 1508
  • 1 508
  • 1,508

There are exceptions though. When you write years, no separations are required:

-This feature was released in 2015.
